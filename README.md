Scores are never adjusted to satisfy an authority. A project scoring 41% on Oversight is reported as 41%. That honesty is the methodology's foundation.

## What AI Does and Does Not Do

**AI does:** Structure project data into a professionally formatted report. Map findings to ADB, IFC, ISO, and GRI framework clauses. Eliminate formatting time so the professional review focuses on substance.

**AI does not:** Visit your site. Verify your data. Apply professional judgement. Take accountability for findings.

**The professional review does:** Verify every finding against submitted data. Check scores for consistency. Apply 22 years of field experience before delivery.

This is how every serious compliance practice operates. The difference is that CM Academy publishes it.
