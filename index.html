Our Process — Fully Transparent

AI-assisted generation.
Professional review. Always.

We believe you deserve to know exactly how your compliance report is produced. This transparency is part of the COS™ methodology — and it is what makes this service trustworthy.

What the automation does

The COS™ Report Generator structures your project data against the requirements of publicly available international frameworks — including the ADB Safeguard Policy Statement, IFC Performance Standards, ISO 45001, and GRI Standards. Each report maps findings to the specific clauses of these frameworks. CM Academy references them as published standards. We are not an endorsed or approved partner of any issuing organisation. Reports prepared by a CCM apply the CMAA Body of Knowledge in professional practice.

What the professional review does

Every report is personally reviewed by Susil Bhandari — founder of the COS™ Methodology, 22 years of international field experience across Nepal, Qatar, and Bahrain — before it reaches you. The review checks that findings are accurate, scores are justified by the data, and the report reflects what is actually happening on site. That review is what makes the report professionally accountable — professional judgement applied to every finding, not just automated generation.

Why this benefits you

A traditional compliance consultant may charge $5,000–$15,000 and take 3 weeks. They also use templates and standard frameworks — just without telling you. Our approach is rigorous in process, transparent in method, and delivered in 24 hours at a sustainable fee that makes mandatory reporting accessible to every project — not only those with large consulting budgets.

Our ethical commitment

The COS™ Methodology is open-access, published on Zenodo (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18802971). Every score is derived from data you provide — not from what any authority wants to see. A report that returns 44% on Oversight is delivered as 44%. We do not adjust scores for approval. That is the difference between compliance reporting and compliance theatre.

What AI Does — and Does Not Do
✓ AI Does

Structure your project data into a professionally formatted compliance report.

Map findings to the relevant clauses of ADB, IFC, ISO, and GRI frameworks.

Eliminate formatting time so the professional review focuses entirely on substance.

✗ AI Does Not

Visit your site or verify conditions on the ground.

Generate assumptions or invent findings.

Apply professional judgement or take accountability for any finding.

✓ The Review Does

Verify every finding against the data you submitted.

Check scores for internal consistency and framework alignment.

Apply 22 years of field judgement before delivery. Named professional accountability on every report.

✓ You Are Responsible For

The accuracy of the data you submit. CM Academy produces a report based on what you provide.

If your data is incomplete or inaccurate, your report will reflect that — and the professional review will flag it.

This division of responsibility is not a disclaimer. It is a description of how every serious compliance practice operates — including large international consultancies. The difference is that we publish it. The COS™ Code of Ethics Principle 3 — Transparent Process — requires nothing less.
